Game Save Backup Tool — portable build
========================================

Extract this folder anywhere (USB drive, D:\Tools, etc.) and run:

  gsbt.exe

No installer required. .NET 8 and the Windows App SDK are already bundled.

Sandbox (optional developer mode)
---------------------------------
  gsbt-sandbox.exe   (same app with sandbox monitor enabled)
  gsbt.exe -s        (equivalent)

Settings and data
-----------------
Same as the installed edition:
  %AppData%\GSBT\winui\

Uninstall
---------
Delete this folder. Your settings in %AppData%\GSBT remain until you remove them.
